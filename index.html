About Us

College, Purulia (Formerly Purulia Government Engineering College) which has been rendering engineering   education since 2016.The college takes pride in the team of its qualified   faculty and staff members who take every effort in mentoring and  transforming the budding students so that they can contribute their best to   the society and to the country with good employability skill and character. Our faculty members help to achieve the dreams of the students and their parents by setting right goals and strategies. We also provide our students ample opportunities for activities like tech fest, cultural fest, sports and games, club activities etc., so that they can discover and excel their hidden talents in areas beyond academics.
